Cutler's RSI.

An indicator of the movement direction and stop.

Indicator Heikin-Ashi Open.

[ColorXDerivative\_HTF](/en/code/21052 "ColorXDerivative_HTF")

Indicator ColorXDerivative with the timeframe selection option available in the input parameters

[BB Stops - MACD](/en/code/21059 "BB Stops - MACD")

MACD indicator that is using very well known BB Stops instead of using signal line for signals.

[Historical Volatility](/en/code/21070 "Historical Volatility")

Historical Volatility (HV) is a statistical measure of the dispersion of returns for a given security or market index over a given period of time. Generally, this measure is calculated by determining the average deviation from the average price of a financial instrument in the given time period.

[CaudateXPeriodCandle](/en/code/21083 "CaudateXPeriodCandle")

Synthetic Japanese candlesticks over the period predefined in the indicator settings, calculating on each bar, averaging the price timeseries used in the indicator, and highlighting caudate candlesticks.

[TII](/en/code/21113 "TII")

Indicator Trend Intensity Index.

[RSI with RSI](/en/code/21125 "RSI with RSI")

The five-week and 17-week RSI values are used for entry and exits while the 17-week RSI is used for trend direction as an entry filter in the retracement entries.

[XPeriodCandle\_main](/en/code/21142 "XPeriodCandle_main")

Indicator XPeriodCandle in the main chart.

[SS](/en/code/21167 "SS")

Indicator SS (Stochastic Stack) represents a set of eight indicators Stochastic with different parameters, united in a single calculation process.

[Pivotal Points](/en/code/21184 "Pivotal Points")

Pivotal points as described in the March 2009 SFO magazine article "Trading FX Like Jesse Livermore Traded Stocks" by Jamie Saettele.

[Rj\_RMA\_HTF](/en/code/21244 "Rj_RMA_HTF")

Indicator Rj\_RMA with the possibility to change the indicator timeframe in its input parameters.

[Zero\_Lag\_TEMA](/en/code/21255 "Zero_Lag_TEMA")

Indicator Zero Lag Triple Exponential Moving Average.

[JB\_Volatility](/en/code/21276 "JB_Volatility")

JB Volatility - market volatility oscillator marking its state in color.

[AbsolutelyNoLagLwma\_Range\_Channel](/en/code/21308 "AbsolutelyNoLagLwma_Range_Channel")

A channel formed by two moving averages AbsolutelyNoLagLwma based on averaged High and Low timeseries

[AbsolutelyNoLagLwma\_Range\_Channel\_HTF](/en/code/21319 "AbsolutelyNoLagLwma_Range_Channel_HTF")

Indicator AbsolutelyNoLagLwma\_Range\_Channel with the timeframe selection option available in the input parameters.

[AB](/en/code/21337 "AB")

Indicator Acceleration Bands

[DirectCandlesCounter](/en/code/21389 "DirectCandlesCounter")

The indicator shows the moment of successive appearance of several unidirectional candles in a row activating alerts and sending email and push notifications

[Pivot Oscillator](/en/code/21395 "Pivot Oscillator")

Variation of a well known Pivot indicator showing pivot lines in a separate window and price position relative to those pivot lines.

[ASH](/en/code/21429 "ASH")

Absolute Strength Histogram (ASH) indicator

[Differential\_Average\_By\_Sultonov\_HTF](/en/code/21455 "Differential_Average_By_Sultonov_HTF")

The Differential\_Average\_By\_Sultonov indicator with the timeframe selection option available in the input parameters.

[AMA three timeframes](/en/code/21465 "AMA three timeframes")

The indicator is displayed using three horizontal lines of iAMA (Adaptive Moving Average, AMA) indicator from three different timeframes.

[QQE Histo](/en/code/21577 "QQE Histo")

Compared to the original QQE indicator, this version is adding fixed levels (to help further estimate the trend) and color changing histogram (based on those levels).

[RSX QQE New](/en/code/21579 "RSX QQE New")

Compared to the original QQE indicator, this version instead of using trailing levels uses fixed levels to estimate overbought and oversold conditions. This version is also using RSX (a smoother RSI without a lag) in order to further purify the signals.

[Price Zone Oscillator](/en/code/21607 "Price Zone Oscillator")

The formula for Price Zone Oscillator (PZO) depends on only one condition: if today's closing price is higher than yesterday's closing price, then the closing price will have a positive value (bullish); otherwise it will have a negative value (bearish).

[Wajdyss\_Ichimoku\_Candle\_Alert](/en/code/21613 "Wajdyss_Ichimoku_Candle_Alert")

Wajdyss\_Ichimoku\_Candle indicator provides alerts, sends email and push notifications when the candles break through the Kijun Sen line (candle color changes to the opposite one).

[PFE\_Overlay](/en/code/21665 "PFE_Overlay")

The Polarized Fractal Efficiency Overlay (PFE\_Overlay) indicator is designed for identifying trends. Unlike Polarized Fractal Efficiency, the indicator is displayed on the price chart as the main indicator line and two lines of the calculated StdDev deviation value.

[YABSI](/en/code/21686 "YABSI")

A signal indicator YABSI. It shows Buy and Sell labels on the price chart.

[TRIX Log](/en/code/21732 "TRIX Log")

TRIX indicator using a logarithm of a price.

[Demand\_Index](/en/code/21740 "Demand_Index")

The Demand\_Index indicator developed by James Sibbet combines the price and volume, and is often considered a leading indicator of price change.

[TD\_I](/en/code/21746 "TD_I")

The TD\_I indicator (Thomas DeMark Indicator) is a modified version of the standard DeMarker Indicator by Thomas R. DeMark.

[AlterTrend](/en/code/21766 "AlterTrend")

The indicator colors candlesticks depending on the trend direction.

[FX Fish 2MA](/en/code/21767 "FX Fish 2MA")

Indicator - trading system "FX Fish 2MA".

[TrendManager](/en/code/21768 "TrendManager")

A trend indicator based on the difference between two moving averages.

[i-AnyRange2Cld](/en/code/21769 "i-AnyRange2Cld")

An indicator of two ranges of arbitrary time intervals.

[i-AnyRangeCldTail](/en/code/21777 "i-AnyRangeCldTail")

An indicator of ranges of arbitrary time intervals with the extension of the interval generated within the specified session until the next session.

[STD Trend Envelopes](/en/code/21821 "STD Trend Envelopes")

Trend Envelopes indicator that uses Standard Deviation for the price change calculation.

[FGDI](/en/code/21826 "FGDI")

Fractal Graph Dimension Indicator
