The EA trades based on the signals of [iIchimoku](/en/docs/indicators/iichimoku "MQL5 Reference → Technical Indicators → iIchimoku") (Ichimoku Kinko Hyo) using a fixed lot. When a signal emerges, opposite positions are closed.

The Buy signal:

```mql5
   if(Senkou_Span_A>Senkou_Span_B)
      if(Close>Open)
         if(Senkou_Span_B<Close && Close<Senkou_Span_A)
```

Example:

![ichimok2005 Open Buy](https://c.mql5.com/18/63/ichimok2005_Open_Buy.png "ichimok2005 Open Buy")

The Sell signal:

```mql5
   if(Senkou_Span_B>Senkou_Span_A)
      if(Open>Close)
         if(Senkou_Span_B>Close && Close>Senkou_Span_A)
```

### Input Parameters

* Ichimoku settings
  + **Ichimoku: period of Tenkan-sen**;
  + **Ichimoku: period of Kijun-sen**;
  + **Ichimoku: period of Senkou Span B**.
* Trade settings
  + **Lots** - position volume;
  + **Stop Loss** (in pips) - stop loss value;
  + **Take Profit** (in pips) - take profit value;
  + **magic number** - Expert Advisor identifier.
