A trading system based on the signals of indicator [FineTuningMACandle](/en/code/22685 "Trend_Intensity_Index - an indicator for MetaTrader 5"). The signal is formed when a bar is closing if there is change in candle direction maintaining change of its color.

For the generated EA to operate correctly, the compiled **FineTuningMACandle.ex5** indicator file should be available in the **<terminal\_data\_directory>\MQL5\Indicators** folder.
