The breakthrough trading system using the [Candles\_Smoothed](/en/code/536) indicator. A signal to perform a deal is formed at bar closing if the breakthrough level is higher than high or lower than low of the indicator candlestick.

Place Candles\_Smoothed.ex5 compiled file to the terminal\_data\_folder\MQL5\Indicators.
