The [Relative Strength Index](https://www.metatrader5.com/en/terminal/help/indicators/oscillators/rsi), applied to standard deviation of price.

It's a simple indicator allows to measure the volatility. It can be applied to determine flat zones by analysis of the [Bollinger Bands](/en/code/14) narrowing and expanding.
