Two Keltner channels based on one moving average, drawn as a colored cloud, with the display of the last values as price labels.

The indicator uses SmoothAlgorithms.mqh library classes (copy it to <terminal\_data\_folder>\MQL5\Include). The use of the classes was thoroughly described in the article [Averaging Price Series for Intermediate Calculations Without Using Additional Buffers](/en/articles/180 "Averaging Price Series for Intermediate Calculations Without Using Additional Buffers").
