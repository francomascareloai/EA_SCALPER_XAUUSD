A simple trend indicator of the oscillator type.

The indicator uses SmoothAlgorithms.mqh library classes (copy it to <terminal\_data\_folder>\MQL5\Include). The use of the classes was described in detail in the article ["Averaging Price Series for Intermediate Calculations Without Using Additional Buffers"](/en/articles/180 "Averaging Price Series for Intermediate Calculations Without Using Additional Buffers").

This indicator was first implemented in MQL4 and was published in [CodeBase](/en/code/10278 "IBS_RSI_CCI_v4") on 04.05.2011.
