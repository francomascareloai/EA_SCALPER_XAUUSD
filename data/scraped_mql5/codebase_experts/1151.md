The Exp\_CoeffofLine\_true Expert Advisor is based on the [CoeffofLine\_true](/en/code/410) indicator. A signal to perform a deal is formed at bar closing, if the histogram breakthroughs its zero level.

Place CoeffofLine\_true.ex5 compiled file to the terminal\_data\_folder\MQL5\Indicators.
