This elegant fan based on the five T3 moving averages strongly resembles [Alligator of Bill Williams](/en/code/9) and is used for market analysis in a similar way.

The indicator uses CT3 class of the SmoothAlgorithms.mqh library. The use of this class is described in the article ["Averaging Price Series for Intermediate Calculations Without Using Additional Buffers"](/en/articles/180).

This indicator was first implemented in MQL4 and published in [Code Base](/en/code/8345) 13.07.2006.

* The smoothalgorithms.mqh library must be placed to the **terminal\_data\_folder**\MQL5\Include.
* The t3taotra.mq5 indicator must be placed to the **terminal\_data\_folder**\MQL5\Indicators.
