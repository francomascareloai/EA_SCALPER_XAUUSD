Trading system using the [MACD\_Xtr](/en/code/1219). A signal to perform a deal is formed at bar closing when a red or a green color bar appears. Exit from the deals is performed either by a pending order, or by bar color changing.

Place MACD\_Xtr.ex5 compiled file to the terminal\_data\_folder\MQL5\Indicators.

Default Expert Advisor's input parameters have been used during the tests shown below. Stop Loss and Take Profit have not been used during the tests.
