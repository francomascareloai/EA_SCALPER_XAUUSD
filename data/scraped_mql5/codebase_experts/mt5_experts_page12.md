[Freeman](/en/code/21616 "Freeman")

Trading by RSI and Moving Average. Moving Average is used as a trend filter on H1.

[Exp\_wajdyss\_Ichimoku\_Candle\_MMRec](/en/code/21612 "Exp_wajdyss_Ichimoku_Candle_MMRec")

[Exp\_Open\_Oscillator\_Cloud\_MMRec](/en/code/21610 "Exp_Open_Oscillator_Cloud_MMRec")

The trading system based on the Open\_Oscillator\_Cloud indicator signals with an ability to change the volume of a forthcoming trade depending on the results of the previous trades for this trading system.

[GBP9AM](/en/code/21595 "GBP9AM")

Working with pending Buy Stop and Sell Stop orders.

[Exp\_DEMA\_Range\_Channel\_Tm\_Plus](/en/code/21558 "Exp_DEMA_Range_Channel_Tm_Plus")

[Crossing Moving Average](/en/code/21515 "Crossing Moving Average")

The EA is based on two iMAs (Moving Average, MA) and iMomentum (Momentum) filter crossing.

[CCI and Martin](/en/code/21511 "CCI and Martin")

The EA based on iCCI (Commodity Channel Index, CCI) indicator plus managing position volumes using martingale.

[One MA EA](/en/code/21499 "One MA EA")

The EA based on single iMA (Moving Average, MA) indicator signals.

[3sma](/en/code/21495 "3sma")

The EA based on three iMA (Moving Average, MA) indicators.

[breakdown](/en/code/21457 "breakdown")

At the beginning of a new day, the EA places pending Buy Stop and Sell Stop orders by High and Low of the previous day.

[ZigZag EA](/en/code/21452 "ZigZag EA")

Expert Advisor based on ZigZag indicator. Working with pending Buy Stop and Sell Stop orders.

[Return Strategy](/en/code/21440 "Return Strategy")

Working with pending Buy Limit and Sell Limit orders. Grid of pending orders.

[Exp\_Ang\_Zad\_C\_Tm\_MMRec](/en/code/21439 "Exp_Ang_Zad_C_Tm_MMRec")

A trading system using the Ang\_Zad\_C trend-following indicator with the possibility to set a strict trading time interval and change the volume of a forthcoming trade depending on the results of the previous trades.

[Exp\_AbsolutelyNoLagLwma\_Digit\_NN3\_MMRec](/en/code/21420 "Exp_AbsolutelyNoLagLwma_Digit_NN3_MMRec")

[OverHedgeV2](/en/code/21411 "OverHedgeV2")

Hedging positions. Working on a new bar.

[Exp\_ColorX2MA\_Digit\_NN3\_MMRec](/en/code/21410 "Exp_ColorX2MA_Digit_NN3_MMRec")

[Exp\_ColorJFatl\_Digit\_NN3\_MMRec](/en/code/21409 "Exp_ColorJFatl_Digit_NN3_MMRec")

Three independent trading systems using ColorJFatl\_Digit indicators within a single EA with an ability to change the volume of a forthcoming trade depending on the results of the previous trades for this trading system

[MostasHaR15 Pivot](/en/code/21394 "MostasHaR15 Pivot")

The EA uses the following indicators: iADX (Average Directional Movement Index, ADX), two iMAs (Moving Average,MA) - one indicator at CLOSE prices and another one at OPEN prices, iOsMA (Moving Average of Oscillator, OsMA).

[Arttrader\_v1\_5](/en/code/21387 "Arttrader_v1_5")

The EA is based on iMA (Moving Average, MA) indicator and analyzes the last six bars.

[CloseProfit v2](/en/code/21384 "CloseProfit v2")

Closing positions and removing pending orders when reaching a specified profit or loss.

[SimplePivot](/en/code/21374 "SimplePivot")

A simple Pivot. No stop loss and take profit are used.

[Urdala\_Trol](/en/code/21311 "Urdala_Trol")

The EA performs initial opening in both directions. Then it tries to pull the remaining side into profit.

[XIT Three Moving Average Cross](/en/code/21697 "XIT Three Moving Average Cross")

Three moving average cross with MACD signal verification before opening order.

Virtual Trailing Stop.

[Exp\_BykovTrend\_ColorX2MA\_MMRec](/en/code/21359 "Exp_BykovTrend_ColorX2MA_MMRec")

Two independent trading systems using indicators BykovTrend\_V2 and ColorX2MA within one EA, with an option of changing the size of the future trade depending on the results of the previous ones.

[cloud's trade 2](/en/code/21348 "cloud's trade 2")

Working with two indicators, Fractals and Stochastic, simultaneously, or with only one of them.

[AOCCI](/en/code/21345 "AOCCI")

An Expert Adviswor based on two indicators: iCCI (Commodity Channel Index, CCI) and iAO (Awesome Oscillator, AO)

[Exp\_GStop\_Tm](/en/code/21343 "Exp_GStop_Tm")

An Expert Advisor for closing all positions, in case of the total profits on those positions exceeded the Take Profit level fixed in inputs or the total losses on those positions exceeded the Stop Loss level fixed in inputs, or where it is necessary to close all positions beyond the time interval fixed in the settings.

[Exp\_GTakeProfit\_Tm](/en/code/21342 "Exp_GTakeProfit_Tm")

An Expert Advisor for closing all positions, in case of the total profits on those positions exceeded the Take Profit level fixed in inputs, or where it is necessary to close all positions beyond the time interval fixed in the settings.

[Exp\_GStopLoss\_Tm](/en/code/21341 "Exp_GStopLoss_Tm")

[Bollinger Band Two MA and ZigZag](/en/code/21300 "Bollinger Band Two MA and ZigZag")

The Expert Advisor uses indicator iBands (Bollinger Bands, BB), two iMAs (Moving Average, MA) from different timeframes, and custom indicator ZigZag.
