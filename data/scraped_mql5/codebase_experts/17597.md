Inverse fisher transform version of nonlag smoothed RSX.

Since RSX is already a "smoother than RSI" RSI, if you wish to turn the nonlag smoothing off, then simple set the nonlag smoothing period to <=1.

This version is extended (compared to MetaTrader 4 version [NonLag Inverse Fisher Transform of RSX](/en/code/17595 "NonLag Inverse Fisher Transform of RSX - indicator for MetaTrader 4")) with a set of 22 price types and a bit different look and feel (using the MetaTrader 5 possibilities).
