Indicator [i-KlPrice\_Vol](/en/code/22688) that shows the direction of moving the histogram bars as colored labels on such bars.

The indicator uses the classes of SmoothAlgorithms.mqh (to be copied to terminal\_data\_directory\MQL5\Include). The use of the classes was thoroughly described in article [Averaging Price Series for Intermediate Calculations Without Using Additional Buffers](/en/articles/180).
