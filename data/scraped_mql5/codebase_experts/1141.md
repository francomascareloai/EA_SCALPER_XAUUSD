The Exp\_CMO Expert Advisor based on signal entry taken from the [CMO](/en/code/784) indicator. A signal to perform a deal is formed at bar closing when the cloud indicator changes its color to the opposite.

Place CMO.ex5 compiled file to the terminal\_data\_folder\MQL5\Indicators.

Default Expert Advisor's input parameters have been used during the tests shown below. Stop Loss and Take Profit have not been used during the tests.
