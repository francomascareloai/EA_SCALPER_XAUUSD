The indicator is a variation of Bollinger Bands and Envelopes, based on two moving averages calculated by the Linear Regression method (LSMA [Linear regression MA](/en/code/20217 "LRL - indicator for MetaTrader 5")).

It has one input parameter:

* **Period** - LSMA calculation period.
