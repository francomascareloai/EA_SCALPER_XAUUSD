Trades three moving averages in trending conditions , opens trades when bar crosses over the smaller moving average.

standard inputs, all modifiable.

buy trades

input 1 ,  5 greater than 20

input 2 ,  20 greater than 30

input 3 , candle crosses above this moving average, 5

input 4 , stop loss in pips and target in % equal to stop.

inputs 5/6/7/8 are in the same order for sell trades.

ability to change moving average type , simple , exponential , and more.
