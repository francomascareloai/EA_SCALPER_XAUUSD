**Filip Radenovski :**   

Can you explain more about the inputs Start, End and Kill, please?

What is their purpose exactly and configuration?

**StartHour** and **EndHour** set the time interval within which the work is allowed.

**KillDay** and **KillHour** additionally set the day of the week (**KillDay**) and the time (**KillHour**) on this day of the week - for the end of the trade: roughly [on Friday](https://www.mql5.com/en/docs/constants/environment_state/marketinfoconstants#enum_day_of_week "MQL5 documentation: Symbol properties") at 9 pm all positions will be forcibly closed.
