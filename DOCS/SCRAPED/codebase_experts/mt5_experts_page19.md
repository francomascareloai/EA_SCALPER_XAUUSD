[DoubleZigZag](/en/code/18629 "DoubleZigZag")

Two ZigZag indicators are used for analysis.

[N Candles v4](/en/code/18609 "N Candles v4")

The Expert Advisor searches for N identical candlesticks in a row. It buys on bullish candlesticks and sells on bearish ones. The account type is taken into account, i.e. whether it is netting or hedging.

[IStochastic\_Trading](/en/code/18584 "IStochastic_Trading")

The Expert Advisor trades based on signals generated by Stochastic Oscillator. Averaging. A limited number of positions. Trailing.

[Intersection 2 iMA](/en/code/18576 "Intersection 2 iMA")

A simple system based on the crossover of two MAs.

[N Candles v3](/en/code/18575 "N Candles v3")

The Expert Advisor searches for N identical candlesticks in a row. It buys on bullish candlesticks and sells on bearish ones.

[Exp\_XROC2\_VG\_X2](/en/code/18526 "Exp_XROC2_VG_X2")

The Exp\_XROC2\_VG\_X2 trend-following trading system is based on the signals of two XROC2\_VG indicators.

[Exp\_XROC2\_VG\_Tm](/en/code/18524 "Exp_XROC2_VG_Tm")

The Exp\_XROC2\_VG\_Digit\_Tm Expert Advisor trades based on signals generated by the XROC2\_VG oscillator.

[EveningStar](/en/code/18507 "EveningStar")

The EveningStar Expert Advisor trades the "Evening Star" pattern. Lot is calculated based on the risk value as a percentage of free margin.

[Bullish and Bearish Engulfing](/en/code/18487 "Bullish and Bearish Engulfing")

The EA trades based on the "Bullish and Bearish Engulfing" pattern.

[VLT\_TRADER](/en/code/18455 "VLT_TRADER")

The trading system is based on volatility compression

[Exp\_NRTR\_ATR\_STOP](/en/code/18448 "Exp_NRTR_ATR_STOP")

The trading system is based on the NRTR\_ATR\_STOP indicator signals.

[exp\_Amstell-SL](/en/code/18443 "exp_Amstell-SL")

The EA buys if the open price of the last position is higher than the current price.

[Crossing of two iMA](/en/code/18440 "Crossing of two iMA")

Crossover of two iMAs (Moving Averages), one more iMA (Moving Average) is used as the filter. Lot: manual or risk percent of balance. Stop, Market or Limit orders. The EA also sets Stop Loss, Take Profit, Trailing Stop.

[Exp\_ColorXMUV\_Tm](/en/code/18434 "Exp_ColorXMUV_Tm")

The Exp\_ColorXMUV\_Tm Expert Advisor is based on the ColorXMUV MA signals and provides the possibility to set a fixed trading time interval.

[Exp\_ColorJFatl\_Digit\_Tm](/en/code/18424 "Exp_ColorJFatl_Digit_Tm")

The Exp\_ColorJFatl\_Digit\_Tm Expert Advisor is based on the ColorJFatl\_Digit indicator signals and provides the possibility to set a strict trading time interval.

[Exp\_PercentageCrossoverChannel\_System](/en/code/18422 "Exp_PercentageCrossoverChannel_System")

The trading system is based on the PercentageCrossoverChannel\_System indicator signals.

[Pipso](/en/code/18333 "Pipso")

The EA trades in the specified time interval. It takes into account High and Low within a certain number of bars. The EA includes protection against the "not enough money" error.

[Fractals minimum distance](/en/code/18326 "Fractals minimum distance")

The EA trades based on the Fractals indicator. Lot calculation: percentage of free margin.

[Exp\_PercentageCrossover](/en/code/18323 "Exp_PercentageCrossover")

The Exp\_PercentageCrossover Expert Advisor is based on the change of the PercentageCrossover color, and provides the possibility to set a strict trading time interval.

[Exp\_TotalPowerIndicatorX](/en/code/18314 "Exp_TotalPowerIndicatorX")

The Exp\_TotalPowerIndicatorX Expert Advisor is based on the signals generated by the TotalPowerIndicatorX oscillator. It also provides the possibility to set a strict trading time interval

[Bollinger Bands N positions](/en/code/18311 "Bollinger Bands N positions")

Signals are generated by the iBands, Bollinger Bands indicator. When a signal is received, opposite positions are closed.

[cheduecoglioni](/en/code/18294 "cheduecoglioni")

The EA waits for a TP or SL to trigger, and then opens a position in the opposite direction. It checks if there is enough money before sending a trade request. OnTradeTransaction.

[All information about the symbol](/en/code/18262 "All information about the symbol")

All information about the current symbol (information from the CSymbolInfo class object is used) represented as a dialog panel.

[RSI and Bollinger Bands](/en/code/18255 "RSI and Bollinger Bands")

A trading strategy based on RSI and Bollinger Bands.

[Vector](/en/code/18251 "Vector")

An Expert Advisor for 4 currency pairs, including EURUSD, GBPUSD, USDCHF and USDJPY. It is based on four-hour moving averages.

[Statistics](/en/code/18164 "Statistics")

Analyzes bars whose opening time (hour and minute) is equal to the current bar opening time.

[Exp\_Kolier\_SuperTrend\_X2](/en/code/18160 "Exp_Kolier_SuperTrend_X2")

This is a trend trading system based on the signals of two Kolier\_SuperTrend indicators.

[Piano](/en/code/18153 "Piano")

Shows an appropriate value for the last three bars on each timeframe: "1" if the bar is bullish, and "0" if the bar is bearish.

[Exp\_XFatlXSatlCloud](/en/code/18134 "Exp_XFatlXSatlCloud")

The Exp\_XFatlXSatlCloud trading system is based on change of the trend direction displayed by the XFatlXSatlCloud indicator

[Exp\_TrailingStop](/en/code/18089 "Exp_TrailingStop")

The Expert Advisor moves trailing stops of all positions on the current symbol.

[Sound Alert Entry Out](/en/code/18082 "Sound Alert Entry Out")

Plays a sound when position is closed.

[Morse code](/en/code/18066 "Morse code")

Trading based on preset candlestick combinations (patterns) Candlesticks are marked as "1" (bullish) and "0" (bearish). TakeProfit, StopLoss

[EXP\_MAX\_LOT](/en/code/18056 "EXP_MAX_LOT")

The Expert Advisor calculates the maximum lot size that can be used to open a position using the entire free margin available on the deposit.

[Exp\_Stochastic\_Chaikins\_Volatility](/en/code/18040 "Exp_Stochastic_Chaikins_Volatility")

The Exp\_Stochastic\_Chaikins\_Volatility Expert Advisor based on the Stochastic\_Chaikins\_Volatility indicator color change.

[Breakdown signaling](/en/code/18024 "Breakdown signaling")

A semi-automated Expert Advisor which signals of breakouts of specified horizontal levels.

[N Сandles](/en/code/18023 "N Сandles")

The EA searches for N identical candlesticks in a row. If the found candlesticks are bullish, it buys. I they are bearish, the EA sells.

[Bollinger](/en/code/17992 "Bollinger")

The Expert Advisor trades inside a channel, reversing positions at the channel borders.

[Candle](/en/code/17988 "Candle")

Trades based on the previous candlestick.

[ListPositions](/en/code/17987 "ListPositions")

A list of all positions. An example of working with CPositionInfo.

[Candle shadow percent](/en/code/17940 "Candle shadow percent")

The Expert Advisor is based on the indicator with the same name. Lot is calculated based on the risk value as a percentage of free margin.
