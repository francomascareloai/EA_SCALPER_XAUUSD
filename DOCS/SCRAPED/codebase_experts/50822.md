MA modified DPO facilitate controlling all parameters of MA (MA period , MA mode , MA applied price) to change required MA curve to horizontal line and can easily used as support or resistance level

![Detrended Price Oscillator - MA modified](https://c.mql5.com/18/155/DPO_modified_screenshot_1.png "Detrended Price Oscillator - MA modified")

[![DPO modified Setup Menu](https://c.mql5.com/18/155/DPO_modified_setup__1.png "DPO modified Setup Menu")](https://c.mql5.com/18/155/DPO_modified_setup.png "https://c.mql5.com/18/155/DPO_modified_setup.png")

Calculation:

> DPO = close - MA (mode, price, DPO\_period)

Where :

> * DPO - DPO indicator value
> * close - close price of bar;
> * mode - applied method to calculate MA (SMA, EMA, SMMA, LWMA)
> * price - applied price to calculate MA
> * DPO\_period - period applied to calculate MA.
