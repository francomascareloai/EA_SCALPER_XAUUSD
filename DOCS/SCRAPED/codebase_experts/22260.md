The trading system based on the signals of the [XDeMarker\_Histogram\_Vol\_Direct](/en/code/22066 "Trend_Intensity_Index - indicator for MetaTrader 5") indicator. Trading signals are formed when a bar closes if the histogram direction has changed (which is displayed by five-pointed stars changing their color).

For the generated EA to operate correctly, the compiled **XDeMarker\_Histogram\_Vol\_Direct.ex5** indicator file should be available in the **<terminal\_data\_directory>\MQL5\Indicators** folder.
