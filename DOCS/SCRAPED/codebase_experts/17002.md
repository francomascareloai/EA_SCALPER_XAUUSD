The Expert Advisor processes signals from four iWPR indicators with different averaging periods: iWPR(26), iWPR(27), iWPR(29), iWPR(30). The OBJ\_TEXT objects are displayed on the chart, keeps a log file.

sig\_buy=iWPRGet(handle\_iWPR\_26,0);  
   sig\_sell=iWPRGet(handle\_iWPR\_27,0);  
   sig\_high=iWPRGet(handle\_iWPR\_29,0);  
   sig\_low=iWPRGet(handle\_iWPR\_30,0);
