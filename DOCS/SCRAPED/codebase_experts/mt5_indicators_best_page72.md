best

[T3](/en/code/424 "T3")

T3 Moving Average is based on multiple exponential smoothing of price.

[ATR Channels](/en/code/494 "ATR Channels")

ATR Channels create price movement channels considering ATR (Average True Range).

[Stochastic CG Oscillator](/en/code/544 "Stochastic CG Oscillator")

Stochastic CG Oscillator is a stochastic oscillator, the values of which are calculated based not on a price series, but on CG Oscillator indicator values.

[StepMA\_Line](/en/code/560 "StepMA_Line")

StepMA is executed as a moving average.

[X2MA NRTR](/en/code/573 "X2MA NRTR")

The hybrid of the universal moving average and NRTR indicator. In this indicator the values of the moving average are corrected with the help of NRTR algorithm (Nick Rypock Trailing Reverse).

[TwoPoleButterworthFilter](/en/code/583 "TwoPoleButterworthFilter")

Two pole Butterworth filter is used for smoothing in this moving average calculation algorithm.

[METRO](/en/code/604 "METRO")

This oscillator displays its values considering RSI (Relative Strength Index) technical indicator.

[VGridLine\_Annual](/en/code/615 "VGridLine_Annual")

Vertical time grid with one year step.

[VGridLine\_Intraday X8](/en/code/620 "VGridLine_Intraday X8")

Vertical time grid with eight hours step.

[SpreadInfo](/en/code/647 "SpreadInfo")

SpreadInfo displays current spread, its average value and their ratio in one of the chart corners.

[XTrendlessOS (trendless oscillator)](/en/code/663 "XTrendlessOS (trendless oscillator)")

The indicator is based on the description given in Joe DiNapoli book "Trading with DiNapoli Levels". It is designed to evaluate the actual market overbought/oversold states.

[ZigZag based on the fractals of a larger timeframe](/en/code/705 "ZigZag based on the fractals of a larger timeframe")

ZigZag based on the fractals of a larger timeframe considering VininI\_FractalsTrend indicator data.

[DinapoliTargets\_Full](/en/code/711 "DinapoliTargets_Full")

This version of the DinapoliTargets\_Full indicator is convenient with its capability to be draw for any bar of a chart, what allows seeing the whole picture of the market behavior relative to the indicator levels on each bar. Apparently this indicator can be most useful when analyzing a strategy in the offline mode.

[FisherTransform\_HTF\_Signal](/en/code/742 "FisherTransform_HTF_Signal")

FisherTransform\_HTF\_Signal shows a trend direction as a graphical object with colored trend indication and gives alerts or audio signals in case of a trend change.

[FlatTrend2](/en/code/757 "FlatTrend2")

The simplest signal indicator for specifying trend power and direction.

[XLineRegression](/en/code/999 "XLineRegression")

XLineRegression creates levels based on the linear regression algorithm.

[MaRsi-Trigger](/en/code/1126 "MaRsi-Trigger")

The Trigger indicator has three states: rising, falling and flat

[Close Flat Channel](/en/code/1133 "Close Flat Channel")

It defines the boundaries of a flat channel based on bars closings.

[True MFI (Alternative to the standard MFI)](/en/code/1154 "True MFI (Alternative to the standard MFI)")

Rise or fall in prices is often preceded by increased activity of trading. The True MFI indicator tries to use this factor.

[MALR](/en/code/1186 "MALR")

The Moving Average of a linear regression with two levels of bands like John Bollinger bands

[Color Parabolic](/en/code/1193 "Color Parabolic")

The iSAR indicator's main advantage is that it is based on current price time series thanks to which it can be applied to the other indicators

[Dserg - LinRegression Breakout](/en/code/1205 "Dserg - LinRegression Breakout")

The indicator for automatic drawing of consolidation areas and channels on the basis of the linear regression

[AnchoredMomentum](/en/code/1233 "AnchoredMomentum")

The improved Momentum indicator. The indicator is described by Rudy Stefenel in February'1998 issue in Stock&Commodities

[2XMA](/en/code/1249 "2XMA")

The MACD histogram drawn using two signal lines in the price chart of the financial asset.

[LeManSystem](/en/code/1263 "LeManSystem")

The system using the internal channel from High to Low

[XXMA](/en/code/1271 "XXMA")

Digital adaptive XXMA Moving Average represents subtype of Moving Averages with a digital flat-trend filter and is made to separate the angular direction of price movement (trend) from the horizontal (flat) for effective trend trading.

[MATonySignal](/en/code/1347 "MATonySignal")

The indicator displays trend force and direction

[Clock](/en/code/1364 "Clock")

The indicator displays three variants of time in the chart: local, server and GMT!

[WcciPatterns](/en/code/1383 "WcciPatterns")

The Woodies CCI Paterns indicator

[WeeklyPivot](/en/code/1572 "WeeklyPivot")

The indicator of reversal points drawn on the week candlesticks

[PairsTrade\_Light](/en/code/1589 "PairsTrade_Light")

The indicator for pair trading. The simplified variant of the "ind\_2\_linep1.mq5" indicator

[CandleSize](/en/code/1647 "CandleSize")

The indicator of ranges of the last three candlesticks of timeframe selected by the trader

[SignalTable](/en/code/1711 "SignalTable")

The original indicator that shows buy and sell signals of three indicators on nine timeframes

[SR\_TLB](/en/code/1753 "SR_TLB")

An interpretation of Stieve Nisson's 'Three Line Break' strategy

[Demo\_IndicatorSetString](/en/code/1760 "Demo_IndicatorSetString")

An example of how to use the IndicatorSetString() function. A simple indicator that shows how to create a label, e.g. "Overbought level" for each level.

[StepChoppy\_v2](/en/code/1882 "StepChoppy_v2")

An indicator of trend power with eight states.

[Stoch\_3HTF](/en/code/1927 "Stoch_3HTF")

Three main stochastic lines from three different timeframes on one chart.

[r\_Gator](/en/code/1943 "r_Gator")

An analogue of the Alligator indicator with a smaller lag of signals.

[FX\_FISH\_2MA](/en/code/2000 "FX_FISH_2MA")

Fisher transformation based histogram with two averages in the form of signal lines.

[DeMarker\_3HTF](/en/code/2013 "DeMarker_3HTF")

Three DeMarker oscillators with three different timeframes on one chart.
