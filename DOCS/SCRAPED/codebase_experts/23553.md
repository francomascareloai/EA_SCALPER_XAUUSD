The EA is a utility. It draws to horizontal lines ([OBJ\_HLINE](/en/docs/constants/objectconstants/enum_object/obj_hline)). It checks once every **Period** minutes, whether the price has crossed one of the lines. If the price crosses the line, a sound alert will be given.
