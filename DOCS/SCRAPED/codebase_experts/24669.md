"Gann Fan" EA draws Gann Fan  on chart and trades with its trend,has Trailing Stop Loss &Take Profit works with all time frames major forex pairs and stocks NASDAQ.

* Try it on demo first.
* This EA trades only with the price of open candle!
* If you do not want to trade with increasing lot size when you lose  a trade set:"IncreaseFactor=0"
* I combined together a simple EA with two moving averages and Gann Fan,
* The Gann Fan do not work on test mode,
* The optimization tests can be performed only with moving averages,
* In live trading only, Gann Fan and moving averages will work together,
* During tests, the EA  only draws the Gann Fan on the screen in  visual mode without considering it in the creation of trades,
* You need to perform back test with "open prices only" option,
* The Gann Fan will function as a filter .
* In this situation the probability of winning trades increases.
