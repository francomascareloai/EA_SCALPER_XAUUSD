# BykovTrend - indicator for MetaTrader 5

Ramdass

**Real author:**

Ramdass - Conversion only

Simple indication of a newly born trend using colored arrows on a chart. The indicator generates its signals based on [Williams' Percent Range (WPR)](https://www.metatrader5.com/en/terminal/help/indicators/oscillators/wpr).

This indicator was implemented in MQL4 and published in [Code Base at mql4.com](https://www.mql5.com/en/code/7247) 28.09.2007.
