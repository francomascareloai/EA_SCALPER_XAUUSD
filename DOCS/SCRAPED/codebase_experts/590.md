This indicator shows the simple clock in comment.

![iSimpleClock indicator](https://c.mql5.com/18/4/1.png "iSimpleClock indicator")

* 1 - Current time.
* 2 - Time, elapsed from bar open.
* 3 - Time to bar close.

The indicator doen't work at W1 and MN1 timeframes.
