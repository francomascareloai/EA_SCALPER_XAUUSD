The main idea of ​​the trading strategy is as follows: when there are three down bars on the current timeframe:

![Billy expert three candles](https://c.mql5.com/18/63/Billy_expert_three_candles.png "Billy expert three candles")

and the main Stochastic line is above the signal line on the other two timeframes:

![Billy expert stochastic](https://c.mql5.com/18/63/Billy_expert_stochastic.png "Billy expert stochastic")

a Buy signal emerges.

The strategy handles only Buy signals.

Testing in the M15 timeframe:

![Billy expert EURUSD M15](https://c.mql5.com/18/63/Billy_expert_EURUSD_M15.png "Billy expert EURUSD M15")

![Billy expert GBPUSD M15](https://c.mql5.com/18/63/Billy_expert_GBPUSD_M15.png "Billy expert GBPUSD M15")

![Billy expert USDJPY M15](https://c.mql5.com/18/63/Billy_expert_USDJPY_M15.png "Billy expert USDJPY M15")

### Input Parameters

* **Lots** - position volume;
* **Stop Loss**
* **Take Profit**
* **Max positions** - the maximum number of open positions;
* **timeframe Stochastic #1** - timeframe for Stochastic #1;
* **timeframe Stochastic #2** - timeframe for Stochastic #2;
* **magic number** - Expert Advisor identifier.
