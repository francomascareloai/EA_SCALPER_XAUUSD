# MQL5 Source Codes of Technical Indicators for MetaTrader 5 - 127

![icon](https://c.mql5.com/i/code/code-icon.png)

MQL5 technical indicators analyze MetaTrader 5 price charts on Forex, as well as stock and commodity markets. Indicators define trend direction and power, overbought and oversold states, support and resistance levels. Underlying mathematical models provide objective assessment of the current market state allowing traders to accept or reject trading system's signals.

You can download and launch offered indicators in MetaTrader 5. The library of indicators is also available directly from MetaTrader 5 platform and MetaEditor development environment.

[J\_TPO\_Velocity](/en/code/763 "J_TPO_Velocity")

Typical unnormalized oscillator displayed as a color histogram.

[KalmanFilter](/en/code/762 "KalmanFilter")

The indicator displays the fast adaptive line that allows to evaluate a trend line.

[JCFBaux](/en/code/760 "JCFBaux")

The oscillator consisting of Momentums series.

[CMO](/en/code/784 "CMO")

In this version of Chande Momentum Oscillator the smoothed price is used for the calculations.

[Ultra Spearman Rank Correlation](/en/code/787 "Ultra Spearman Rank Correlation")

This trend indicator is based on SpearmanRankCorrelation indicator values and analysis of its signal lines.

[ColorStdDev](/en/code/761 "ColorStdDev")

Most visually convenient trend power indicator based on Standard Deviation.

[JBrainTrend1](/en/code/755 "JBrainTrend1")

Trend indicator of the popular BrainTrend1 trading system with preliminary price timeseries smoothing.

[XR-squared](/en/code/752 "XR-squared")

XR-Squared indicator uses linear regression to determine the presence or absence of a market trend.

[DSS Bressert](/en/code/789 "DSS Bressert")

The Double Smoothed Stochastics indicator was proposed by William Blau and Walter Bressert. The calculation of DSS values is similar to Stochastic indicator, the difference is the use of double exponentially smoothing.

[UltraFatl](/en/code/788 "UltraFatl")

This indicator is based on Fatl indicator values and analysis of its signal lines.

[WKBIBS](/en/code/750 "WKBIBS")

WKBIBS is a next generation oscillator with combined WKB and IBS indicators functions.

[Sidus](/en/code/751 "Sidus")

This indicator is based on the first version of Sidus trading method. It shows market entry points.

[TrendManager](/en/code/754 "TrendManager")

Visual trend indicator that shows the direction and power of the current price movement.

[Ultimate\_Oscillator](/en/code/792 "Ultimate_Oscillator")

The Ultimate Oscillator by Larry Williams is calculated as weighted value of three Stochastic, indicators, calculated for fast, middle and short periods.

[IBS](/en/code/749 "IBS")

Internal Bar Strength Indicator measures the "inner strength" of each bar by subtracting the close price from the low and dividing the resulting number by the difference between high and low bars.

[X2MA\_KLx3\_Cloud](/en/code/739 "X2MA_KLx3_Cloud")

Keltner Channel displayed as colored background.

[FisherTransform\_HTF\_Signal](/en/code/742 "FisherTransform_HTF_Signal")

FisherTransform\_HTF\_Signal shows a trend direction as a graphical object with colored trend indication and gives alerts or audio signals in case of a trend change.

[HLR](/en/code/791 "HLR")

The HighestLowestRange (HLR) determines the relative price position in range of the several X bars ago. If price located at range bottom (new low), the indicator is equal to 0, if price position at the top of the range (new high), the indicator is equal to 1 (or 100%).

[MPC](/en/code/790 "MPC")

The MPC indicator plots a simple channel using extermums of the specified period. It can be used for additional visual control of trading system (channel breakout), based on HighestLowestRange (HLR) indicator.

[X2MA\_BBx3\_Cloud](/en/code/738 "X2MA_BBx3_Cloud")

Bollinger Bands displayed as colored background.

[UltraCCI](/en/code/740 "UltraCCI")

This indicator is based on CCI (Commodity Channel Index) and the analysis of a trend direction of its multiple signal lines.

[BBSqueeze](/en/code/745 "BBSqueeze")

Unnormalized symmetrical oscillator based on the linear regression algorithm with the simplest trend power indicator.

[Ultra Momentum](/en/code/741 "Ultra Momentum")

This indicator is based on Momentum and its multiple signal lines analysis.

[XMA Ichimoku Channel](/en/code/733 "XMA Ichimoku Channel")

Envelope construído pelas médias móveis e calculado da mesma maneira que os de Ichimoku Kinko Hyo.

[LinearRegSlope\_V1\_HTF\_Signal](/en/code/744 "LinearRegSlope_V1_HTF_Signal")

LinearRegSlope\_V1\_HTF\_Signal shows a trend direction as a graphic object with colored trend indication and sends alerts or audio signals in case the trend changes.

[UltraXMA](/en/code/730 "UltraXMA")

This indicator is based on the integral value of the trend from the fans of similar moving averages with an arithmetic progression of the smoothing period.

[UtterFractals](/en/code/736 "UtterFractals")

This indicator shows the "inner market structure" close to the real one in contrast to the standard "fractals" and all sorts of zig zags.

[ZigZag\_NK\_MTF](/en/code/737 "ZigZag_NK_MTF")

ZigZag indicator version displaying results of its calculations from the larger timeframe at the current one.

[iGDR\_Fractal\_Levels](/en/code/729 "iGDR_Fractal_Levels")

iGDR\_Fractal\_Levels shows the fractals average values over a certain period of time.

[Waddah Attar Fibo](/en/code/731 "Waddah Attar Fibo")

The indicator displays possible support and resistance lines as colored dots using Fibo levels.

[3XMA\_Iсhimoku](/en/code/735 "3XMA_Iсhimoku")

The indicator that reminds of Ichimoku Kinko Hyo by its creation and usage principles.

[HL Cross Signal for WPR](/en/code/728 "HL Cross Signal for WPR")

The HLCrossSig for WPR is considered as a trend indicator, since it is intended for "catching" trends. However, it's a pretty complex and informative indicator that allows excluding the psychological and emotional influence from trading.

[XMA\_Ichimoku](/en/code/734 "XMA_Ichimoku")

Moving average with calculation algorithm equivalent to the one of Ichimoku Kinko Hyo.

[UltraWPR](/en/code/722 "UltraWPR")

This indicator is based on WPR (Larry Williams' Percent Range) and its multiple signal lines analysis.

[FractalLevels](/en/code/732 "FractalLevels")

FractalLevels displays the channel based on fractals.

[^X\_NonLinearRegression](/en/code/743 "^X_NonLinearRegression")

Non-linear regression with interpolation of a price chart future values.

[ColorXWPR](/en/code/753 "ColorXWPR")

Enhanced version of Larry Williams' Percent Range.

[Past Regression Deviated](/en/code/717 "Past Regression Deviated")

Channel trend indicator.

[UltraRSI](/en/code/725 "UltraRSI")

This indicator is based on RSI and its multiple signal lines analysis.

[MA\_AC\_Stochastic\_Signal](/en/code/720 "MA_AC_Stochastic_Signal")

This indicator shows trend direction (Moving Average) and trading signals (Stochastic + Accelerator).
