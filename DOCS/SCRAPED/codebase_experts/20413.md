The Indicator ZeroLAG MA was described in journal Technical Analysis of Stocks and Commodities, April, 2000 for the first time and according to creators, the Indicator ZeroLAG MA is a Moving Average with zero delay.

This indicator uses TEMA calculation mode in order to make the lag lesser compared to the original [Zero lag MA](/en/code/20411 "Zero lag MA - indicator for MetaTrader 5"), and that makes this version even faster than the [Zero lag DEMA](/en/code/20412 "Zero lag DEMA - indicator for MetaTrader 5") too.
