[ADX System](/en/code/17030 "ADX System")

The Expert Advisor is based on the signals of the iADX (Average Directional Movement Index) indicator.

[Exp\_Fractal\_ADX\_Cloud](/en/code/17027 "Exp_Fractal_ADX_Cloud")

The trading system based on crossing of the DI+ and DI- lines of the Fractal\_ADX\_Cloud indicator.

[PROphet](/en/code/17022 "PROphet")

The PROphet Expert Advisor consists of two independent linear perceptrons.

[MA Cross](/en/code/17021 "MA Cross")

The Expert Advisor based on intersection of two iMA.

[Elite eFibo Trader](/en/code/17015 "Elite eFibo Trader")

Grid with a constant step. Grid lot sizes based on Fibonacci.

[Very Blonde System](/en/code/17012 "Very Blonde System")

The Expert Advisor opens a reverse position, additionally placing a grid of orders for reinforcement.

[escape](/en/code/17009 "escape")

The Expert Advisor trades based on two iMA: iMA(5) and iMA(4) on the M5 timeframe.

[Exp\_Fractal\_WPR](/en/code/17006 "Exp_Fractal_WPR")

The simplest EA based on the fractal WPR.

[The MasterMind 3](/en/code/17002 "The MasterMind 3")

The Expert Advisor processes signals from four iWPR indicators with different averaging periods.

[VR---STEALS-2](/en/code/17000 "VR---STEALS-2")

The Expert Advisor manages positions using labels.

[The MasterMind](/en/code/16995 "The MasterMind")

The Expert Advisor uses the iStochastic (Stochastic) Oscillator, iWPR (Larry Williams' Percent Range) indicators.

[Bollinger Bands](/en/code/16994 "Bollinger Bands")

The Expert Advisor opens trades based on the Bollinger Bands indicator.

[Exp\_Zonal\_Trading](/en/code/16993 "Exp_Zonal_Trading")

The Expert Advisor which uses the AO and AC indicators in its operation, which were proposed by the Bill Williams.

[Stochastic Three Periods](/en/code/16987 "Stochastic Three Periods")

Uses the iStochastic (Stochastic) indicator from three different timeframes.

[Stoch](/en/code/16983 "Stoch")

Trades using the pending Sell Limit and Buy Limit orders. Removes all orders and closes positions at 23:59.

[Exp\_ColorZerolagMomentum\_X2](/en/code/16981 "Exp_ColorZerolagMomentum_X2")

The Exp\_ColorZerolagMomentum\_X2 trend trading system based on the signals from two ColorZerolagMomentum indicators.

[GO](/en/code/16968 "GO")

Signals based on four iMA (MA) indicators, by PRICE\_OPEN, PRICE\_HIGH, PRICE\_LOW, PRICE\_CLOSE prices. Trades only on hedge accounts.

[Expert MACD EURUSD 1 Hour](/en/code/16967 "Expert MACD EURUSD 1 Hour")

Works based on the iMACD (MACD) indicators

[Exp\_2pbIdealMA\_ReOpen](/en/code/16958 "Exp_2pbIdealMA_ReOpen")

The Exp\_2pbIdealMA EA is based on the crossing of Moving Averages with position scaling according to the trend.

[CrossMA](/en/code/16949 "CrossMA")

Intersection of two iMA (MA). Uses the iATR indicator.

[Exp\_FisherTransform\_X2](/en/code/16947 "Exp_FisherTransform_X2")

The Exp\_FisherTransform\_X2 trend trading system based on the signals from two FisherTransform indicators.

[Exp\_Fractal\_RSI](/en/code/16938 "Exp_Fractal_RSI")

The simplest EA based on the fractal RSI.

[Exp\_AdaptiveCGOscillator\_X2](/en/code/16937 "Exp_AdaptiveCGOscillator_X2")

The Exp\_AdaptiveCGOscillator\_X2 trend trading system based on the signals from two AdaptiveCGOscillator indicators.

[Frank Ud](/en/code/16932 "Frank Ud")

Works only on the hedge accounts! Martin, martingale. Doubling the lot at loss.

[TrendMeLeaveMe](/en/code/16921 "TrendMeLeaveMe")

The semi-automated Expert Advisor using a trend line (TrendLIne).

[Simplest Hedging EA ever](/en/code/16920 "Simplest Hedging EA ever")

Only for hedge accounts. Opening two opposite positions on a new bar.

[Expert NEWS](/en/code/16899 "Expert NEWS")

Expert NEWS - MetaTrader 5 Expert Advisor. BuyStop and SellStop pending orders.

[VR---SETKA---3](/en/code/16898 "VR---SETKA---3")

VR---SETKA---3 - MetaTrader 5 Expert Advisor. This is a continuation of VR---SETKA . The EA is based on the martingale principle. Grid. Martingale. Use only on hedge accounts.

[Exp\_JBrainTrend1Stop\_ReOpen](/en/code/16894 "Exp_JBrainTrend1Stop_ReOpen")

The Exp\_JBrainTrend1Stop\_ReOpen trading system is based on the JBrainTrend1Stop indicator color change with adding to trend-following positions.

[DoubleUp с изгибом](/en/code/16886 "DoubleUp с изгибом")

The Expert Advisor works comparing iCCI and iMACD indicators.

[Opening and Сlosing on time v2](/en/code/16885 "Opening and Сlosing on time v2")

The Expert Advisor opens and closes at a certain time comparing two iMA indicators having different periods. A trade having a specified volume is opened at a specified symbol. Setting Stop Loss and Take Profit. Select position type: Buy or Sell.

[Exp\_Bezier\_ReOpen](/en/code/16883 "Exp_Bezier_ReOpen")

The Exp\_Bezier\_ReOpen trading system is based on the Bezier indicator color change with adding to trend-following positions.

[Simple FX](/en/code/16877 "Simple FX")

The Expert Advisor is based on moving averages crossing.

[Exp\_ColorJFatl\_Digit\_ReOpen](/en/code/16872 "Exp_ColorJFatl_Digit_ReOpen")

The Exp\_ColorJFatl\_Digit\_ReOpen trading system is based on the ColorJFatl\_Digit indicator color change with adding to trend-following positions.

[Exp\_Fishing](/en/code/16868 "Exp_Fishing")

The Expert Advisor opens an initial position in the current candle's direction and increases its volume if profit in points increases a threshold fixed in the EA inputs.

[Exp\_WPR](/en/code/16867 "Exp_WPR")

The simplest Expert Advisor based on WPR.

[Original Turtle Rules Trader](/en/code/16866 "Original Turtle Rules Trader")

Original Turtle Rule Trader Expert Advisor implements a trading system described in the book "The Original Turtle Trading Rules". The EA code implements the visual display of the three Donchian channels, money management, opening and adding trades and moving stop levels.

[CandelsHighOpen](/en/code/16863 "CandelsHighOpen")

CandelsHighOpen Expert Advisor is based on the Candels High Open indicator trading signals module. The EA features trading market and pending orders, as well as trailing stop based on Parabolic SAR.

[Exp\_MFI](/en/code/16856 "Exp_MFI")

The simplest Expert Advisor based on MFI.
