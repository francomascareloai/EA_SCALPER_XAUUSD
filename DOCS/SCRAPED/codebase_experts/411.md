**Desciption:**

The Chande Momentum Oscillator (CMO) was developed by Tushar Chande and is a technical indicator that attempts to capture the Momentum. Chande discussed this and many other indicators in his book ["The New Technical Trader: Boost Your Profit by Plugging into the Latest Indicators"](/go?link=https://www.amazon.com/New-Technical-Trader-Plugging-Indicators/dp/0471597805/ "http://www.amazon.com/New-Technical-Trader-Plugging-Indicators/dp/0471597805/"). Тhe range of CMO is from -100 to 100. The values +50 and -50 are assumed as ovebought and oversold states of the market.

The indicator uses the SmoothAlgorithms.mqh smoothing algorithms library (SmoothAlgorithms.mqh must be placed to **terminal\_data\_folder**\MQL5\Include), described in the article ["Averaging Price Series for Intermediate Calculations Without Using Additional Buffers"](/en/articles/180).
