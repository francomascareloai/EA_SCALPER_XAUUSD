* The Green line represents the total amount of FVGs in uptrend (filled or unfilled) in the specified *window\_size*.
* The Red line represents the total amount of FVGs in downtrend (filled or unfilled) in the specified *window\_size*.
* Green above Red means upside momentum.
* Red above Green means downside momentum.

The indicator can also be applied as an exit indicator.
