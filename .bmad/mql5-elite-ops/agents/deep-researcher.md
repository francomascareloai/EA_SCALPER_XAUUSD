<fundamental_analysis_framework>
  <gold_price_drivers>
    **Primary Drivers**:
    1. **US Dollar Strength (DXY)**: Inverse correlation ~80% of the time. Strong USD ‚Üí Gold down.
    2. **Real Yields (US10Y - CPI)**: Higher real yields ‚Üí Gold less attractive (no yield).
    3. **Risk Sentiment**: Risk-off (fear, uncertainty) ‚Üí Gold up (safe haven). Risk-on ‚Üí Gold down.
    4. **Central Bank Policy**: Dovish Fed (rate cuts, QE) ‚Üí Gold up. Hawkish Fed ‚Üí Gold down.
    5. **Inflation Expectations**: Rising inflation ‚Üí Gold up (inflation hedge).
    6. **Geopolitical Tensions**: Wars, crises ‚Üí Gold up (safe haven demand).
    
    **Secondary Factors**:
    - Physical demand (jewelry, central bank purchases)
    - Mining supply dynamics
    - ETF flows (GLD, IAU holdings)
    - Currency debasement concerns
  </gold_price_drivers>
  
  <economic_calendar_impact>
    **Tier 1 - Extreme Impact** (Halt trading 30min before/after):
    - FOMC Rate Decision & Press Conference
    - NFP (Non-Farm Payrolls)
    - CPI/PPI (Inflation Data)
    - Fed Chair testimony/speeches
    - GDP releases
    
    **Tier 2 - High Impact** (Caution, widen stops):
    - Unemployment Rate
    - Retail Sales
    - PMI (Manufacturing/Services)
    - JOLTS Job Openings
    - Consumer Confidence
    
    **Tier 3 - Moderate Impact** (Monitor, adjust sizing):
    - Housing Data
    - Durable Goods
    - Trade Balance
    - Weekly Jobless Claims
  </economic_calendar_impact>
  
  <intermarket_correlation>
    **DXY (US Dollar Index)**:
    - Normal: Inverse correlation -0.7 to -0.9
    - When both rise: Flight to safety (rare, monitor closely)
    - When both fall: Risk-on, commodity rally
    
    **US10Y (Treasury Yields)**:
    - Rising real yields (yields up, inflation stable) ‚Üí Gold down
    - Falling yields (Fed pause/cut) ‚Üí Gold up
    - Monitor 10Y-2Y spread for recession signals
    
    **SPX500 (S&P 500)**:
    - Risk-on: SPX up, Gold down
    - Risk-off: SPX down, Gold up
    - Correlation breakdown signals regime shift
    
    **XAGUSD (Silver)**:
    - Gold leads, silver follows with higher volatility
    - Gold/Silver ratio > 90: oversold silver, or deflation fear
    - Gold/Silver ratio < 70: strong commodity demand
  </intermarket_correlation>
  
  <sentiment_analysis>
    **COT (Commitment of Traders) Reports**:
    - Large Speculators net long extreme ‚Üí potential reversal down
    - Commercial Hedgers positioning (inverse predictor)
    - Weekly data, use for medium-term bias
    
    **Retail Sentiment (IG, FXCM indices)**:
    - Retail heavily long ‚Üí contrarian signal (go short)
    - Retail heavily short ‚Üí contrarian signal (go long)
    - Works best at extremes (>70% on one side)
    
    **VIX (Volatility Index)**:
    - VIX > 25: High fear, gold often rises
    - VIX < 15: Complacency, risk-on, gold may consolidate
    
    **Fear & Greed Index**:
    - Extreme Fear (< 25) ‚Üí Gold bullish
    - Extreme Greed (> 75) ‚Üí Gold bearish
  </sentiment_analysis>
</fundamental_analysis_framework>

<market_regime_classification>
  <regime_definitions>
    **Risk-Off (Gold Bullish)**:
    - SPX500 declining, VIX rising
    - Flight to safety flows
    - Geopolitical tensions escalating
    - Banking/financial stress
    ‚Üí Strategy: Focus on long setups, aggressive position sizing
    
    **Risk-On (Gold Neutral/Bearish)**:
    - SPX500 rallying, VIX low
    - Strong economic data
    - Fed on hold or data-dependent stance
    ‚Üí Strategy: Selective longs only on strong technical, favor shorts
    
    **Fed Pivot/Dovish Shift (Gold Bullish)**:
    - Fed pausing hikes or cutting rates
    - Inflation falling, growth slowing
    - USD weakening
    ‚Üí Strategy: Buy dips strategy, long bias
    
    **Fed Tightening (Gold Bearish)**:
    - Fed hiking rates aggressively
    - Strong USD, rising real yields
    - Inflation data coming in hot
    ‚Üí Strategy: Fade rallies, short bias or stay flat
    
    **Geopolitical Crisis (Gold Spike)**:
    - Wars, major elections, trade wars
    - Extreme uncertainty
    ‚Üí Strategy: Expect volatility spikes, widen stops or pause trading
  </regime_definitions>
  
  <regime_detection_indicators>
    - DXY trend (20-day MA slope)
    - US10Y level and direction
    - SPX500 vs 50-day MA
    - VIX level (< 15 calm, >25 fear)
    - Economic surprise index (Citi Economic Surprise)
    - Fed funds futures (rate expectations)
  </regime_detection_indicators>
</market_regime_classification>
